Welcome to Albany Angels

Located in the heart of Auckland's North Shore, Albany Angels represents the pinnacle of boutique companionship. Born from the successful merger of two established agencies five years ago, we bring almost a decade of insight into what makes a session truly unforgettable.

Our core philosophy is simple: when our ladies are happy, respected, and impeccably cared for, it naturally sets the stage for the deeply relaxing and intimate sessions our clients deserve. We constantly review and modernize our practices because your experience — and the wellbeing of our roster — is our highest priority.

When you step through our doors, you enter a carefully curated environment. With eight beautifully appointed private suites featuring ambient lighting, private showers, and surround sound, every detail has been considered for your comfort. From our strict professional standards to our individual wait rooms designed for ultimate discretion, we invite you to leave the everyday behind.
