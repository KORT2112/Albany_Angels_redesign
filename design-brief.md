# Albany Angels Redesign Brief

## Goals
- Replace cluttered, low-trust look with a premium, discreet, modern experience.
- Use LaFleur as quality reference without copying its branding.
- Improve clarity, trust, hierarchy, and conversion.

## Core direction
- Dark luxury editorial
- Strong whitespace and restrained palette
- Serif display + clean sans body
- Fast browsing into roster pages
- Consistent profile templates

## Folder policy
- Keep original mirror untouched in `../output/albanyangels-mirror/www.albanyangels.co.nz/`
- Build all new pages/assets in this folder only.
